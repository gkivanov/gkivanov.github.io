Summary

Hello! I am a software engineer at Bloomberg LP.

The mobile revolution is just beginning and is on the path of becoming a reflection of everybody's outer, real-world life. Users, being the center stage, are experiencing mobile in ever-increasing new ways built around their daily needs, such as transport, payments and information.

My work is focussed on creating end-to-end solutions for utilising Bloomberg's outstanding content and high performance data delivery systems to present users with financial information, news and insights from across the world at the comfort of their mobile devices.

My other interests include machine learning, blockchain technologies and financial markets.
